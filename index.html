Faith, Courage, Love, Support

Love To Be There is a faith based organization geared to helping those who are less fortunate. We provide things such as clothes, food,toiletries etc. There's alot of people around the world and in the City of Decatur who say they stand for helping those who have none but don't do anything about it. Here at this organization, we want those who have less or are poor to feel comfortable, and know that they are cared for by the hands of giving. What better way to start showing that care, support and love than in your own Community!
